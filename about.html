About APARA

APARA (Asia Pacific Assistive Robotics Association) is a young association founded on the motivation to help users leverage digital technology to augment human capability and potential. In particular, our team studies aspects of the ethical and responsible use of AI and robotics applications. Our membership comprises both users and suppliers of technology in the industry. We collaborate with counterpart industry associations with a common cause as well as research institutes and universities on practical research projects.
