Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 15 July 2025

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you interact with our website www.apara.asia. We are committed to maintaining your trust by handling your data in accordance with international data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (LGPD).

2. What Data We Collect

We collect personal data that you voluntarily provide through the following channels:

  • FormSubmit Forms – used for inquiries, contact, and registrations
  • Google Forms – used for surveys, event sign-ups, and feedback
  • Email links (mailto) – direct contact initiated by users

The data we may collect includes (but is not limited to):

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Company or organisation
  • Country or region
  • Any additional information voluntarily submitted

3. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • Responding to your inquiries or messages
  • Managing feedback, registrations, and communication history
  • Providing updates or information relevant to your request

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your data under one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Your explicit consent (e.g., submitting a form)
  • Legitimate interest (e.g., responding to inquiries)
  • Compliance with legal obligations

5. Your Rights (GDPR, CCPA, LGPD)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Request a copy of your data (data portability)
  • Object to the processing of your personal data
  • File a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email provided in Section 10 below.

6. Data Collection and Storage Platforms

We use third-party tools to handle form submissions and communications:

  • FormSubmit: Submissions made via HTML forms are securely emailed to us through FormSubmit’s platform. FormSubmit does not store message content permanently.
  • Google Forms: Data submitted is stored securely within Google’s infrastructure, governed by Google’s Privacy Policy.
  • Email Communication: Emails are received and stored securely in our mailbox hosted on a protected platform with encryption and spam filtering.

7. International Access and Transfers

Because our services are accessible globally, your data may be transferred and processed in countries outside of your residence. All processing complies with applicable laws and safeguards are taken to protect data during transmission, especially when using Google and FormSubmit services.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, accidental loss, or destruction. While no online platform can guarantee absolute security, we take reasonable steps to maintain a secure environment.

9. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies, web beacons, analytics platforms, or any form of behavioural tracking. Your browsing data is not collected or stored during your visit to this site.

10. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external platforms such as LinkedIn, Facebook, or YouTube. These platforms operate under their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them before interacting with their content. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites.
